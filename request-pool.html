Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to post a request? ▼

Yes! Posting a request is completely free. You only pay when you accept an offer and the service is completed.

How do I know if a provider is reliable? ▼

Each provider has a rating, review count, and experience level displayed. You can also message them before booking to ask questions.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service? ▼

Contact our support team immediately. We have a dispute resolution process to protect both buyers and providers fairly.

How do providers earn money? ▼

Providers earn the price they quote when their offer is accepted. Earnings are paid securely through Vora after service completion.

Can I cancel a request? ▼

Yes, you can cancel an open request anytime before accepting an offer. Once you accept an offer, you may have cancellation terms depending on the provider.
